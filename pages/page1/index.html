"It doesn't really matter what you do just do it perfectly." — René Lacoste, Founder of Lacoste

Founded in 1933 by French tennis legend René Lacoste, the brand quickly became a symbol of timeless elegance and athletic innovation. René, known as “The Crocodile” for his tenacity on the court, created the iconic crocodile logo that still defines the brand today. René revolutionized tennis apparel by designing the first polo shirt made from breathable piqué cotton. It became a favorite among athletes and fashion enthusiasts alike, blending performance with style and embodying casual luxury.

René Lacoste was not only a champion athlete, but also a visionary. He revolutionized the world of tennis apparel by designing the first-ever polo shirt with a breathable fabric called piqué cotton, which was both lightweight and comfortable, offering the perfect balance of performance and style. This polo shirt quickly became a favorite, not just for athletes, but also for those who appreciated quality craftsmanship and sophistication. The classic Lacoste polo, with its embroidered crocodile logo, became a symbol of casual luxury.

In the decades that followed, Lacoste expanded beyond tennis wear to include a broad range of fashion and lifestyle products, from footwear to fragrances, eyewear, and accessories. Throughout, the brand maintained its commitment to combining sport with elegance, offering timeless designs with modern updates that felt both fresh and functional.

Lacoste’s influence grew globally, becoming a recognizable name in over 120 countries. It stayed true to its core values of quality, innovation, and an active lifestyle, constantly evolving to meet the needs of modern, fashion-conscious individuals who appreciate both sophistication and comfort.

Today, Lacoste is a lifestyle that blends sport and elegance, worn by athletes and individuals worldwide. With new collections, collaborations, and a focus on sustainability, the brand stays true to its heritage while innovating for the future. Committed to ethical production and sustainable materials, Lacoste continues to make a positive impact. Celebrate over 90 years of confidence, sophistication, and sport.
